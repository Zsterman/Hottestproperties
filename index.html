About Bradley

Born and raised in New York, Bradley Sterman relocated to North Carolina in 2005 with his wife Linda and two sons Zack and Brandon.
After gaining 36 years of experience in the fine Jewelry industry and owning a Jewelry wholesale business, he has transferred those essential skills and genuine love for helping others into cultivating a successful career that he truly enjoys - Real Estate.

Bradley brings a wealth of knowledge in both negotiations and the contract-to-close process. As someone who has purchased and sold a plethora of homes, he can relate to the stress involved with moving and understands what is needed to keep home buyers and sellers in the loop.

When Bradley isn't working, he's spending time with his family, playing guitar with his sons, or taking long walks with his wife and cute pup Luna.
